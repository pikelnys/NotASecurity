NOT A SECURITY

"An investment contract is an investment of money in a common enterprise with a reasonable expectation of profits to be derived from the entrepreneurial or managerial efforts of others." - SEC

Not A Security (NOT) is not an investment contract. It is a wealth-redistribution utility token.

No common enterprises, no entrepreneurial or managerial efforts.

No reasonable expectation of profits - the expected value of buying NOT is 0.

How it works:

  • Buy NOT at a constant 1:1 exchange rate with Ethereum.
  • All token sale proceeds go to the top 10 token holders.
  • The more NOT you own, the greater your portion of the proceeds.
  • NOT tokens are ERC20 compliant
